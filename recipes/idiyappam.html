Idiyappam Recipe

A south Indian Breakfast dish

Idiyappam, also known as string hoppers, is a traditional South Indian dish made from rice flour. It is a popular breakfast or dinner option, often served with coconut milk and sweetened with jaggery or sugar. Here's a simple recipe to make idiyappam at home.

Ingredients:

  • Rice flour (roasted): 2 cups
  • Water: 2 ½ cups (adjust as needed)
  • Salt: ½ tsp
  • Oil: 1 tsp (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough

    • Heat water
    • Mix dough
    • Cool slightly

  2. Shape the idiyappam

    • Use an idiyappam press
    • Prepare the steamer
    • Form strings

  3. Steam the Idiyappam

    • Steam cook
    • Cool and serve
